Manifesto

Conheça nossa ideologia.

We, as the Yara Digital Farming team, have the AMBITION to transform Yara and the markets. Our CURIOSITY about the world and its coming changes will lead us to drive Yara and the industry towards innovation.

We believe in COLLABORATION as a tool to transform and connect, because we are ACCOUNTABLE for the actions we take together, towards this journey we have joined, and will ultimately contribute to our responsibly feeding the world and protecting the planet. We will get the TRUST through delivering what we promised, and we expect the according reciprocity as well. The OPENNESS for real talks about our accomplishments and failures we face along the way, will help us to foster an innovative organization, after all, every failure is also an opportunity to learn, because we are Yara, and knowledge grows.

We have the EMPOWERMENT to take decisions and move our agendas forward, always in a SWIFTLY way, but always without trading of the QUALITY of our work. We are the Yara Digital Farming team, and we are to envision and create the innovative solutions that build the transformation at Yara and in the world!
